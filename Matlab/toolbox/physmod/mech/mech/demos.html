SimMechanics Demos

SimMechanics simulates translational and rotational motion of rigid bodies connected together by joints and actuated by forces and torques.

The demos are listed in this table from simpler to more advanced. They will acquaint you with some of the features of SimMechanics and how you can use SimMechanics with other MathWorks products.

  • SF = Demo model uses Stateflow module but does not require Stateflow to be installed
  • VR = Demo model can render the machine in a virtual scene that requires Virtual Reality Toolbox to view

Pendulum Models

Simple Pendulum: A rod swinging by one revolute joint

Double Pendulum: Two connected rods swinging by two revolute joints

Double Pendulum with Stiction: Two swinging rods slowed by sticky friction

Automotive Models

Crash Test Dummy: Animated VR automotive crash test with front seat crash dummy

Clutch Mechanism: SimMechanics and Simulink automotive clutch models compared

Four Cylinder IC Engine: Internal combustion engine with four cylinders, SF logic, and VR animation

Transmission: Detailed model of an automotive gear box with SF logic

Motorcycle Linear Stability: Interactive linearized stability analysis of motorcycle motion (interact at the command line)

Motorcycle Nonlinear: Full nonlinear motorcycle motion

Stewart Platform Models

Stewart Platform: Three-legged Stewart platform with predefined trajectory

Stewart Platform: Three-legged Stewart platform with slider bar control

Stewart Platform: Three-legged Stewart platform with VR joystick control (requires external joystick)

Other Models

Ballistic Motion with Kinetic Friction: Projectile motion with air drag

Rolling Sphere: Sphere rolling in two dimensions with rolling constraints between translation and rotation

Point-Curve Constraint: Body constrained to move along a toroidal curve

Bouncing Ball: Ball bouncing off the ground with penalty force model for impact

Rack and Pinion: Rack and pinion assembly mechanism

Four Bar Mechanism: Three rotating bars in a closed loop with ground

Flyball Governor: Mechanical governor with shaft, collar, arms, and balancing balls

Planetary Gears: Animated VR planetary gear set

Conveyor Mechanism: Conveyor loader and belt delivery system with slider bar control

Conveyor Mechanism: Conveyor loader with slider bar control and VR animation

Robot Arm: Detailed model of a realistic robot with its controllers and VR animation
