GPIO A MUX Control

GPAMUX

     BIT           Peripheral Name (bit = 1)             GPIO Name (bit = 0)

EV-A Peripheral                    

     0                    (PWM1)                                GPIOA0

     1                    (PWM2)                                GPIOA1

     2                    (PWM3)                                GPIOA2

     3                    (PWM4)                                GPIOA3

     4                    (PWM5)                                GPIOA4

     5                    (PWM6)                                GPIOA5

    

     8                    (QEP1)                                  GPIOA8          

     9                    (QEP2)                                  GPIOA9
