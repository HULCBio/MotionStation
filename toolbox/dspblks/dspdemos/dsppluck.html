Simulation of a Plucked String

This is a simulation of a plucked string that employs digital waveguide synthesis. An on-line textbook, entitled Digital Waveguide Modeling of Musical Instruments by Julius O. Smith III is available, last we checked, on the web. and covers significant background related to digital waveguides.

The Harmony Central website also provides very useful background information on a variety of related topics.

This Simulink implementation is based largely on an original M-file implementation available from Daniel Ellis, at his web site.
