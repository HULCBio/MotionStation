Filter options

The Filter options panel allows you to specify what kinds of objects to look for and where to search for them.

Look for

The Look for list lists the types of Simulink and Stateflow objects that the Finder can find. Click on the expand button (+) next to the product name (Simulink and, if installed, Stateflow) to see a list of object types. By unchecking a type, you can exclude it from the Finder's search.

Look inside masked systems

Checking this option causes the Finder to look for objects inside of masked subsystems.

Look inside linked systems

Checking this option causes the Finder to look for objects inside subsystems linked to libraries.
